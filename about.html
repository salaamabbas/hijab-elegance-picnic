Our Story

More than a picnic — it's a sisterhood

Every year, Salaam Safaris brings sisters together for the Hijab Elegance Picnic — an Islamic-based gathering built on friendship, faith and fun. While rooted in Islamic tradition, every sister is welcome regardless of religion, because the memories we create belong to all of us.

Each edition carries its own dress theme, told entirely through hijab styling — from a picnic bursting with colour, to an edition dressed head to toe in black, to elegant black-and-white, to the earthy prints of Derah. Beyond the styling, the heart of the day stays the same: drawing sisters closer, and gently promoting the elegance of Islamic dressing.

What happens at the picnic

The day opens in reflection, grounding everyone present in faith and calm before the festivities begin.

A guided Islamic teaching session shared with warmth, offering lessons sisters carry home with them.

Open, honest conversation circles where friendships form and stories are shared freely.
