Edition 04

The Derah Edition

📅 21 September 2025 📍 Great Habib Resort, Kasangati 🎙️ Guest of Honour: Hajat Shania Kigozi

The Story

Our most textured edition yet

Held on 21 September 2025 at Great Habib Resort in Kasangati, the fourth edition introduced Derah — a hijab fabric known for its striking animal-print and tie-dye designs — as its theme. Sisters arrived dressed head-to-toe in Derah, turning the resort grounds into a runway of rich texture and earthy pattern.

The day followed the tradition sisters have come to expect — Quran recitation, a Darsu session, sister talks, team-building games and a shared picnic spread — made extra special by the presence of guest of honour Hajat Shania Kigozi.

The Derah Edition pushed the picnic's styling furthest yet, proving that Islamic modest dressing can be playful, textured and fashion-forward, all without losing its elegance.

Hajat Shania Kigozi
