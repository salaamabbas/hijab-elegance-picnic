The Story

Where the tradition began

The very first Hijab Elegance Picnic set the tone for everything that followed. Sisters gathered at KCCA Gardens in Kamwokya dressed in every colour imaginable, turning the lawn into a joyful, vibrant sea of hijabs.

The day opened with Quran recitation and a Darsu session, grounding the gathering in faith before sisters moved into team-building games, open sister talks, and a shared picnic spread. It was simple, warm, and colourful — and it proved that a themed hijab gathering could become something sisters would look forward to every year.

This edition planted the idea that would grow into Salaam Safaris' signature format: one shared theme, told entirely through the elegance of modest dressing.
