Edition 02

The Black Edition

📍 KCCA Gardens, Kamwokya 🖤 Theme: All-black elegance

The Story

Bold, striking, unmistakably elegant

Returning to KCCA Gardens in Kamwokya, the second edition took a bold turn — every sister dressed entirely in black. What could have felt sombre instead became a powerful statement: modest dressing can be striking, confident, and beautiful all at once.

The rhythm of the day stayed familiar and grounding — Quran recitation, a Darsu session, sister talks, team-building games and a shared picnic spread — but the sea of black hijabs against the green gardens gave the edition its own unforgettable identity.

The Black Edition proved that Salaam Safaris' themed format could carry real creative range, setting up the more elaborate themes that followed.
